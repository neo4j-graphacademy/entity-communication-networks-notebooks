Subject: Translation - Nikkan Kogyo 5/21 (Enron Mentioned) 
To date, reports on Enron's role in California have been limited to a reprint 
of a Sacramento Bee article appearing in the Denki Shimbun - a limited 
circulation publication sponsored by the Japanese utilities. Last Friday Mr 
Ohta, President of Chubu Electric and Chairman of the Federation of Electric 
Power Companies, made some uninformed comments at a regular energy press club 
briefing about Enron's commitment to its Californian customers . Ohta will be 
retiring from both positions in two weeks. 
In the past both Chubu Electric and the Federation of Electric Power 
Companies have distanced themselves from Mr Ohta's public comments which are 
generally considered to be ultra conservative and unsupported by facts. As a 
result, we have tended to ignore his comments. However, on this occasion I 
think we need to set the record straight on Enron's role in California. I 
would welcome your thoughts on whether we should let the comments pass or 
respond.
