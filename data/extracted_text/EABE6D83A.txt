IMPORTANT NOTICE - Change of G-IMB 
The attached was posted on GasSelect. 
Please make sure you read so that you 
become familiar with the new standby procurement penalty rules for the G-IMB 
tariff. 
In a nut shell: 
1) Penalty is now calculated at 150% of highest daily border price index at 
the SoCal border. 
2) The charge should begin from the first day of the "flow month" and end five 
days prior to the start of each corresponding imbalance trading period. 
3) 
The charge will be posted at least one day in advance of each 
corresponding imbalance trading period. 
Since this became effective on June 1, SoCalGas will determine the highest 
daily border price during the period of June 1 through July 20 and post it no 
later than July 24. 
Customers are assessed this penalty for any 
underdeliveries outside the 10% tolerance for trades that take place from July 
25 -30 for June imbalances. 
Please call me if you have questions regarding this new G-IMB calculation.
