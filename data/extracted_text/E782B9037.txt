‘Total Reqs':

and dates

in the columns
labelled
"NOM".
The
Enron schedulers will want
to check this
sheet
to
see how much gas
is needed
at
a specific point.
The AGL volumes
are not timely and should be ignored until
further
notice.
CES
schedulers
note:
the
attached
file
is
archived

P:\Energy Ops\Enron\April_00\Daily\

"Daily Change'
:
shows the change
(increase +/decrease
-)
in the daily volume
needed from Enron at the designated citygate delivery point
relative to the
supply volumes
arranged previously with Enron prior to the current date.
For
the current gas

the previous day's volume request

gas

3/28,

3/27
request

3/28.
Note that this procedure assumes that the prior day supply change was
in
fact effectuated).
For tomorrow's
gas day and any subsequent

shown,
the
change
is computed as the
latest Dth Nomination requirement
less the
First-of-the-month

(future)
days
shown.
(e.g.,
If
today
is
3/28,
then the change for 3/29,
3/30,
etc.

to

purchases
or sell-backs
