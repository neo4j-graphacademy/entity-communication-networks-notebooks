Administration Energy Policy
Unlike the past Administration where Secretary Richardson served as the
lead
on energy policy,
the Bush Administration has created
a new,
interagency
group to develop energy policy.
"The National Energy Policy Development
Group"
was created for this purpose and will be
lead by Vice President
Cheney with former Senate Energy Committee
Staff Director Andrew Lundquist
serving as the group's director.
As
such,
rather than focusing our policy
meetings
at
DOE,
we should refocus
instead on developing policy with this
group which includes
DOE,
DOA,
DOI,
Treasury,
etc.
Brian Malnak
is the new
Senate Energy Committee
Staff Director.
