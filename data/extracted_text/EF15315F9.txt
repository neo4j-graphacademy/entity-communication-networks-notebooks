RE: Proposed SCE Negotiation Strategy 
Couple of additional pieces of information: 
In conjunction with the issue paying ESPs past due PX amounts, Edison has 
affirmed that it will be making a proposal regarding how it wants to calculate 
the PX credit going forward. 
It remains unclear whether Edison will try to fold controversial issues into 
negotiations on getting paid past due PX credit amounts(e.g., PX credit 
contributed to undercollection and therefore DA customers should help pay down 
the undercollection; DA customers must pay for stranded DWR contracts, etc.). 
So while Edison is making good noises about wanting to settle (which is a 
certainly a step in the right direction), it's still unclear whether they 
intend to attach terms to the deal that make the whole thing a nonstarter. 
Got a call into John Fielder to try to get answers to some of these questions. 
Will report back when (and if) we get some answers. 
Best, 
Jeff
