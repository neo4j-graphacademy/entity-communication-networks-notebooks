At the conclusion of yesterday's meeting, I felt there was still some room for 
us to clarify the situation with Harbor. 
I have summarized below what the 
Volume Management team concluded the process will be on a go-forward basis 
(starting in May): 
1) 
On the 2nd to last business day, Chris Foster will enter two annuities for 
the gas transaction. 
The 1st annuity is for the West Gas to sell to West 
Services and the 2nd annuity is for West Services to sell to Harbor. 
The 
amount of this annuity is based on the most current number Vivan Chen provides 
Chris and has zero financial affect on the WestServices book. 
2) 
On the last business day of the month, Risk calcs the month's transactions 
taking into account the gas annuities and the Forward power (deals which are 
entered on a daily basis). 
Also included is the ISO Actualization for a prior 
month as well as an annuity entered by Chris Foster to account for the West 
Services shared savings for this prior month. 
-this shared savings calc is from a spreadsheet that takes into 
account all the costs that Harbor informs Chris Foster of 
and will never 
be a loss to the West Services book 
3) 
20th of following month, update both gas annuities by production month 
4) 
25th of following month, send 2 separate invoices/payments sent to Harbor 
for 1) gas and 2) forward power.
