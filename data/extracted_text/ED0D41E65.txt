the investigative arm of Congress will <b>sue Vice President Dick 
Cheney</b> over his <b>refusal to turn over information</b> related to 
the work of his <b>energy task force</b>. 
The GAO wants to know with 
whom the task force met, especially within the energy industry. 
The 
<b>White House insists the request is inappropriate</b> and promises to 
put up a fight. 
On Thursday from the Washington bureau, we'll hear 
where this dispute is headed.

to make an appearance at both</b>.
