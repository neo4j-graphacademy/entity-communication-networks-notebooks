PSIM: A Power Simulation Tool 
PSIM is a proprietary model developed by Enron Research Group. 
? 
It takes Power, Gas, Weather and demand information into consideration 
and 
uses Monte-Carlo simulation to assess the expected deal value and risk 
distribution. 
? 
It evaluates complex electricity related contracts such 
as Full Requirement 
and load following contract. 
?

management deals. 
?

various types 
of contracts, explain the model structure and point out further applications.
