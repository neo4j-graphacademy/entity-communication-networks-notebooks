        ?       ?       STAMFORD, Conn. (VENTUREWIRE) -- Walker Digital, an
incubator for Internet ?startups and creator of priceline.com, laid off all

relations at Walker until ?this week's lay-offs. Ms. Zaremba said the company
will stop funding four ?startups: Pulse, Atlantis, HighCircle, a recruitment
services firm, and ?RetailDNA, a commerce technology company. Walker Digital's
founder Jay Walker ?could not be reached for comment. The company's investors
included General ?Atlantic Partners, Allen & Co., Bain & Co., Vulcan, and
Goldman Sachs.?  http://www.walkerdigital.com?   ?       Healthcare Tech Firm
SoftWatch Lays Off 20% of Staff?
        NEW YORK  (VENTUREWIRE) -- SoftWatch, which develops and sells
healthcare
industry technology, said it laid off 30 employees, or 20% of its staff, in
its New York and Israel offices. The Tel Aviv office was the strongest hit by
the layoffs, with 22 positions eliminated, and the New York office eliminated
eight positions, all in the professional services sector. The company said
the layoffs were a result of its shift in business strategy, stating that it
plans to sell through third party integrators and alliance partners. Despite
the layoffs, the company said it will continues to hire in its research and
development and sales departments. The company said it expects a 2.5%
increase over last year's revenues. SoftWatch is funded by Warburg, Pincus
Equity Partners, ABS Capital Partners, Bedrock Capital, and MedScape.
        http://www.softwatch.com
